## REVIEWER_NOTES

Bazidpur is a private community and heritage app for families connected to
Bazidpur village (Nawada district, Bihar, India). The app is account-gated:
sign-up requires admin approval before a user can post or view member-only
sections. The sign-in credentials above belong to a pre-approved member.

CONTENT MODERATION (Guideline 1.2)
- User-generated sections — Forum (threads and replies), Photo Albums, and
  Video Albums — include both a flag-to-report (⚑) on every post and a
  long-press-to-block on every author name or card. Reports go to a
  database table reviewed by our admin team and acted on within 24 hours.
  Blocked members can be reviewed and unblocked from the user's Profile
  screen ("Blocked Members").
- Admins resolve each report by choosing one of three documented actions:
  "Reviewed — no action needed", "Warning issued to member", or "Member
  suspended". Choosing suspend flips the offender's role and prevents
  sign-in. Every resolution is recorded against the report row with the
  resolving admin's id, a timestamp, and optional internal notes — visible
  in the "Resolved" tab of the Flagged Content screen as a full audit
  trail.
- When an admin acts on a report, the original reporter automatically
  receives an email summarising how their report was handled — one of
  three tailored messages matching the action above. Admin internal notes
  are never included; the reporter only sees the outcome and the reason
  they originally filed.
- Editorial sections — Timeless Moments, Rhymes & Roots (Poetry), Memoirs,
  Reading Room, and the public Media screen — are curated and uploaded by
  the Bazidpur admin team only. A small banner at the top of each of these
  screens makes this clear to members. The "report" and "block" controls
  do not appear there because the content has already been editorially
  selected and there is no other member to block.
- A "Community Guidelines" link is on the sign-up screen alongside the
  Privacy Policy. Both must be accepted before an account is created,
  with the acceptance timestamps stored in the user record.

ADMIN FEATURES
- The Admin tab visible inside the app links out to the web dashboard at
  https://bazidpur.com/admin for content moderation, member approval, and
  report triage. This is intentional — the demo account is a member, not
  an admin, so the Admin tab will not appear during review.

ACCOUNT DELETION
- Members can delete their account from Profile → Sign Out → Delete
  Account, or by writing to the admin team via the Contact screen. We
  process deletion requests within 7 days.
